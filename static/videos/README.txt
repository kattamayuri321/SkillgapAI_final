HOW TO ADD YOUR VIDEO:

1. Record or prepare your demo video (MP4 format recommended)
2. Name it exactly: demo.mp4
3. Place it in this folder (static/videos/)
4. The video player will automatically find and play it!

Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264) or WebM
- Resolution: 720p or 1080p
- Duration: 1-3 minutes recommended
- File size: Under 50MB for best performance

For detailed instructions, see: HOW_TO_ADD_VIDEO.md in the project root.
